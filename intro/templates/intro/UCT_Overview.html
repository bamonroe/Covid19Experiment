Thank you for joining the study! Recall:

  • The study should take about 2 to 3 hours of your time to complete. You may do this all in one sitting or at different intervals over the 24-hour period communicated in your invitation email. However you choose, it must be fully completed for payment. If you take a break, we suggest that it would be best if you take it between tasks rather than during a single task.
  • Within the 24-hour period, you can pick back up where you left off by clicking the link sent in the invitation email. You may also revisit that link any time after you complete the study to see a copy of the receipt sheet. The online receipt contains no personally identifiable information and only displays after you complete the study.
  • A participation payment of R{{ participant.vars.endowment }} will be paid to you after completion of the study.
  • There are 2 questionnaires and 4 incentivised tasks associated with this study.
  • In addition to the participation payment for completing the study, each incentivised task has the potential to get you additional earnings either today or in the future. The amount of additional earnings depends on your responses, chance, and realised outcomes.
  • All 4 incentivised tasks will be explained through instruction videos before you participate.

The last item we need from you before beginning the study is your cellphone number so you can receive the participation payment and any additional earnings from the incentivised tasks through Standard Bank Instant Money. You will receive an SMS from Standard Bank with your Instant Money voucher number, and a separate SMS with your code to activate the voucher. The voucher can be redeemed for cash at Spar supermarkets, Standard Bank ATMs, and other stores. See the Standard Bank Instant Money website for more details. Note that your instant Money voucher will expire 14 days after you receive it, so please make sure you redeem the voucher for cash within 14 days.
